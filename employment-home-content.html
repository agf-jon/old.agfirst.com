AgFirst Profile

Located in downtown Columbia, S.C., AgFirst has approximately 340 employees and is the largest financial institution headquartered in the state.

AgFirst’s primary business is to provide funding, operational support and technology services to 19 Agricultural Credit Associations in 15 eastern states and Puerto Rico. These associations, operating as Farm Credit and Ag Credit associations, provide real estate and production financing to 80,000 farmers, agribusinesses and rural homeowners. AgFirst reported assets of more than $27 billion as of March 31, 2014.

AgFirst also operates a growing Capital Markets unit that arranges and participates in loans for agribusinesses across the nation and a Correspondent Lending department that buys, sells and services rural home loans.

Diversity at AgFirst

AgFirst believes that an inclusive team of employees is the best way to serve our agricultural and agribusiness customers. AgFirst refers to our diversity initiatives as inclusion initiatives. With inclusion, AgFirst seeks to involve all employees, our management team, and our board of directors in fostering an environment that encourages and acknowledges the positive contributions individuals from all backgrounds can make in the success of our organization. By embracing and leveraging our unique strengths we establish ourselves as a positive corporate citizen and an employer of choice.
