Don W. Freeman of Montgomery, Alabama, manages a 400-acre cow-calf operation and an 80 unit river rental business near Lowndesboro, Alabama. He is a member of the national Farm Credit Council Board, Lowndes County Farmers Federation Board, and the Lowndes County Cattlemen’s Association Board. He is also past president of the Alabama Chapter of the American Society of Farm Managers and Rural Appraisers. Mr. Freeman is a member of the Board Audit Committee.
