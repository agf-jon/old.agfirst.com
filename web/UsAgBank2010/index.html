Welcome to the registration site for the U.S. AgBank, FCB 2010 Annual Stockholders Meeting. The links to the left provide you with all the tools you need to successfully register for the meeting.

We look forward to seeing you in Tucson, Arizona!

Online Registration: Both your meeting registration and hotel reservation are to be completed through this website and are due on or prior to Friday, February 12.

A participant should first determine if registrations will be handled entirely by your Association, or if you will complete your registration on your own. 

  • If you are completing registration on your own, please select the Online
    Registration link.

  • If your Association is coordinating registration, please select the Paper Registration link and print, complete, and return the Meeting Registration Form and Lodging Request Form to your Association. The appropriate staff will take the information from both forms and reserve your guest room and space for all functions (meetings, meals, social functions and/or tours).

Meeting Information: For an agenda, meeting details, and information about Tucson, Arizona, please select this link. This is a booklet provided in PDF format.

Airport Transportation: Complimentary airport transportation is being offered on Monday and Tuesday, March 15 & 16, preceding the meeting, and Thursday and Friday, March 18 & 19, following the meeting. To make a reservation, please select this link. The due date to secure your reservation is Monday, March 1. Reservations made after this date are not guaranteed and are subject to space availability.

Change or Cancel Registration: To make changes to, or cancel your Annual Stockholders Meeting Registration or Hotel Reservation please select this link. You will receive an email within 72 hours confirming your cancellation. After Monday, March 1, all registrations – including “no shows” – are subject to the full attendee registration fee and/or spouse/guest registration fee, plus any tours in which your spouse/guest has registered.

If you or your spouse/guest are unable to attend the meeting or a tour, a substitute from your Association may attend in your place.

 

Statement of Recognition: Attention CEOs – Please select this link to complete a Statement of Recognition form. This information is requested from you on or prior to Friday, February 12.
