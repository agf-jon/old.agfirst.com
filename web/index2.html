AGFIRST FARM CREDIT BANK

AgFirst Farm Credit Bank is part of the Farm Credit System, the largest
agricultural lending organization in the United States.

With assets of more than $24 billion, AgFirst provides funding and financial services to 23 farmer-owned financial cooperatives in 15 eastern states and Puerto Rico. These cooperatives, operating as Farm Credit and Ag Credit agricultural credit associations, provide real estate and production financing to 80,000 farmers, agribusinesses, and rural homeowners.
