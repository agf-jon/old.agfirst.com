INFORMATION MANAGEMENT DEPARTMENT BUSINESS DISCLOSURES

Availability

The Information Systems Network Infrastructure System is available in accordance with established service level agreements which are documented on our Intranet.  Availability is monitored by the Network System Services team that assists customers with availability problems.  Availability reports are provided to customers, through our Intranet, on a monthly basis.

In the event of an outage, redundancies are built into the infrastructure to allow for automatic failovers to backup devices.  To reduce the risk of lost data and provide for data restoration, backups are periodically rotated off-site.  Additionally, a documented Disaster Recovery Plan is in place and tested at least annually.

Security

Documented security policies are made available internally on our Intranet.  Any intrusion of the computer systems, networks or data resources is responded to and managed by the AgFirst Security and Incident Response Team.  This team is responsible for monitoring security and developing and implementing security awareness training programs and new policies.

Firewalls, virus protection and system monitoring tools are used to help the Security and Network System Services personnel monitor the environment for any unauthorized access attempts. 

Annually all employees must read and sign an acknowledgement stating they have attended security awareness training which addresses security responsibilities and obligations.

Confidentiality

All customer data processed, managed, maintained or stored by AgFirst shall be treated in a manner consistent with existing policies and procedures relating to confidentiality as documented in AgFirst Service Level Agreements. The annual Security Awareness training emphasizes the importance of protecting customer data and adhering to policies as it relates to security and confidentiality.
