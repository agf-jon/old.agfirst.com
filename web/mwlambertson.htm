M. Wayne Lambertson of Pokomoke City, Maryland, owns and operates with his son a 2,700-acre farm of corn, soybeans, and wheat, and a 300,000 capacity broiler operation. He is co-owner of a restaurant, Green Turtle, and partner in a development and construction company, J.W.L. Enterprise, LLC. He currently serves on the national Farm Credit Council Board, MidAtlantic Farm Credit, ACA board of directors and the board of the Delmarva Poultry Industry DPI, a trade organization. Mr. Lambertson serves as chairman of the Board Governance Committee.
