William C. Bess, Jr., from Lincolnton, North Carolina, is owner of Bess Farms. Inc., a grain and hay production operation and has a 70-head cow-calf operation. He serves on the boards of the National Farm Credit Council Board, the Farm Credit System’s national trade organization, Farm Credit Council Services, and Carolina Farm Credit, ACA. He is also a member of the Cleveland County and Catawba Cattlemen’s Associations. Mr. Bess is a member of the Board Governance Committee.
