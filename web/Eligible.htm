You don't have to be a full-time farmer to borrow from Farm Credit. If you derive even part of your income from the production of an agricultural commodity, own agricultural land, or live in a rural area or in a town with a population of 2,500 or less, you may be eligible to borrow from Farm Credit.

(Eligibility requirements are established by Congress through the Farm Credit Act.)

Please contact us to discuss your specific situation by calling 1-800-845-1745, extension 506., or use our online form.
