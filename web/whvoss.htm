William H. Voss, is from McComb, Mississippi. He has commercial cattle and timber operations in Southwest Mississippi and is involved in land and commercial property management. His career includes production agriculture, agribusiness and real estate. He currently serves on the Board of directors of First South Farm Credit, ACA. He is a former agricultural commodities and securities broker and has served as Chairman of the Mississippi Real Estate Commission and Chairman of the Pike County Farm Service Committee. Mr. Voss serves as a member of the Board Compensation Committee.
