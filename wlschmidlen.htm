Walter L. Schmidlen, Jr., from Elkins, West Virginia, is a past dairy farmer and now continues his cow-calf operation along with growing hay and corn on a 700 acre farm with lease/rented land. He is owner/operator of a farm equipment business. He presently serves on the board of the Farm Credit of the Virginias, ACA, and was a former director of Southern States Cooperative and Sire Power. Mr. Schmidlen serves as a member of the Board Governance Committee.
