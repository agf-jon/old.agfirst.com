Eugene W. Merritt, Jr., from Easley, South Carolina, is co-owner of an ornamental tree farm and is a landscape contractor. He also operates a 400-acre timber and grass farm. He serves on the boards of AgSouth Farm Credit, ACA; People Bancorp, a commercial bank holding company; Peoples National Bank, a commercial bank; and Jackson Companies, a recreational company. Mr. Merritt serves as chairman of the Board Compensation Committee and is a member of the AgFirst/FCBT Plan Fiduciary Committee.
