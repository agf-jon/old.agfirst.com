EMPLOYMENT OPPORTUNITIES

Welcome to AgFirst Farm Credit Bank and thank you for considering employment opportunities with our organization.

Farm Credit has been serving the credit needs of agriculture for more than 95 years. Our chartered service area is one of the most stable and profitable in the Farm Credit System. We provide financial services to agricultural credit associations in North Carolina, South Carolina, Georgia, Florida, Alabama, Louisiana, Mississippi, Ohio, Tennessee, Kentucky, Delaware, Maryland, Pennsylvania, Virginia, West Virginia, and Puerto Rico.

AgFirst Farm Credit Bank is dedicated to providing quality services to its customers, as well as an excellent work environment for its employees. AgFirst places great value on its employees, as reflected in a variety of ways: 

  • Competitive compensation
  • Top-rated benefits program
  • Training and development opportunities
  • Advancement opportunity through internal job postings

All of these factors contribute to an organization with high morale, many long-term employees, and strong team spirit.

We appreciate your interest in employment opportunities with AgFirst Farm Credit Bank and wish you success in your career.
